Making Mini bricks, making the mold.

For this week, the assignment was molding and casting. While I got trained on the shop bot device, my mold wasnt working correctly so I decided to 3d print it. This would create the mold that would then be used with silicone to create a cast. A picture of the cad image as well as the 3d printed mold is below.

For the next part, I poured silicone into the tray and let it cure overnight.

  • As you see in the tray, I poured silicone all over the tray and waited overnight for it to cure. Because the design was so small, I had a hard time removing the silicone even after using the silicone-removing methods. Eventually, I had to just salvage what I could, and take the silicone cast out. Though it was meant to be another mold itself for smaller bricks, none of the brick holes were firm enough for small bricks to be produced well enough. Thus, I left this project at that..
