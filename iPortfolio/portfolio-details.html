Final Project Idea

The final project I hope to implement is a variation of a wearable fashion tech line, with 3 beauty/fashion items to complete. Inspired by Billy Porter's moving hat, Doja cat's moving clown outfit, and Cameron Hughes's motion sensored feather dress, I hope to combine fashion and function in 3 unique items.


Idea 1: Wearable bluetooth speaker earrings

  • As the name suggests, the first concept that I have is creating fashionable earrings, perhaps designed shaped like people, mini record players, or even an animal/insect of choice, that can play music as you walk through the streets. These would be cut by the laser cutter and hopefully designed to be lightweight.

    • Idea 2: Skirt that lights up in the dark

  • Similarly, I thought of this concept for performing artists, or those who want to be bold and fashionable while walking or dancing at night. The skirt would sense the light, and the darker the surrouding, the brighter the skirt lights up. This would likely be done with a potentiometer.

    • Idea 3: Scarf with programmable moving feathers

  • This idea is most closely related to the feather dress on the left. I'm still thinking on how the movement would be controlled, but it would likely by able to be controlled by the wearer as a scarf sculpture piece..
